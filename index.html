About

Hi! I’m Sung-Lin Yeh (葉松霖), a second-year PhD student at ILCC, the school of informatics, University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Hao Tang.
I'm interested in speech and language processing and machine learning. In particular, I focus on self-supervised learning, learning discrete and structured speech representations in the language of probabilistic graphical models.
I’m also a developer of the SpeechBrain project, working with Mirco Ravanelli and Titouan Parcollet.
